English Kids Program Launch Plan

Yakuin & Nishijin Locations - Schedule & Implementation Strategy

Program Structure

Frequency & Make-Up Rules

  • Base Rule: Once per week per child
  • Fallback System:

Yakuin Monday (if) full → Thursday

Nishijin Tuesday (if full) → Friday

Grade Bands

  • Lower: Grades 1-3
  • Upper: Grades 4-6

17:00-18:00 (G4-6)

Note: FC owner approval needed for Nishijin Tue/Fri off-peak schedule

Capacity & Scaling

Initial Capacity

  • Per block: Start with 6 kids (safe & coachable)
  • Duration: 60 minutes per block
  • Daily blocks: 2 per location

Growth Strategy

  • After testing: Consider possible Scale to 8 → 10 → 12 kids/block
  • Overflow solution: Saturday rotation

If day 1 and day 2 full(example, Monday and Thursday):

•Week A: Yakuin Saturday

•Week B: Nishijin Saturday

Operational Design

Staffing Efficiency

  • One English Kids coach covers both sites
  • Coach off-days: Wednesday & Sunday

Strategic Scheduling

  • Yakuin kids classes before adult evening classes (Mon/Thu)
  • Nishijin on Tue/Fri avoids cross-site conflicts

Launch Timeline

Flyer Adjustments: 2 days

Distribution: 10 days posting window

Launch Schedule

Nishijin (January 2026)

Strategic Considerations

Why Avoid December Start (Nishijin)

  • Family travel & year-end holidays disrupt routines
  • School winter break changes schedules
  • Competition from intensive winter courses
  • Year-end party season reduces focus
  • Reduced gym hours (Dec 29 - Jan 3)
  • January = Fresh start mindset for parents

Success Factors

  • Trial Response: Expect inquiries 3-5 days after posting
  • Main Wave: Weeks 2-4 after distribution
  • Payment Start: Classes begin week after trial week
  • Focus Opportunity: Consider adult program development at Yakuin through October
